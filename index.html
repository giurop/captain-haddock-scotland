Captain Haddock
Adventures in
Scotland

The journey to this faraway land has started when Capitain Haddock and Tintin received a letter from Her Majesty the Queen to report that her precious crown had been stolen. After a lot of searching and fighting, they finally located it in a remote Scottish island.

Now, they are heading back to the castle to hand back to her in person...

Game Rules

Scottish whisky comes in handy when you could use some extra strength! Look out for the water, it's not good for your health and will cause you some damage. Also, if you are lucky enough to spot Nessie, she is a sweetpie and will give you a heart.

Just don't let the enemies get too close to Tintin and Snowy!
